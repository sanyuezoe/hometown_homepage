Visit Taiyuan

Enjoy culture in this ancient city close to the capital city.

Top three activities to do at Taiyuan

Feel the Glory of Jin State in Jinci Temple

Walking in Jinci Temple, you can see murals and statues several hundred years ago and trees several thousand years ago.

Have a Deeper Understanding of Taiyuan in Shanxi Museum

Shanxi Museum is the cultural relics collection, protection, research and exhibition center of Shanxi Province.

Explore Colorful Taiyuan Nightlife

Liuxiang is one of the most popular places for people to come after work. There are busy cafes and bars that are open to the midnight.
